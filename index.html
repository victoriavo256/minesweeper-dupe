The rules of minesweeper are simple: avoid the bombs!

  • Select a tile to reveal what's hidden underneath.
  • A numbered tile indicates the number of bombs in the tile's proximity.
  • Avoid the bombs and reveal all the safe tiles to win!


Good Luck!
