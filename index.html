The rules are of minesweeper are simple: avoid the mines!


Select a tile to reveal what's hidden underneath. If there's a bomb, it's game over.


Sometimes, the tile you click on will have one or more bombs in their direct proximity.

If this is the case, the tile will have a number indicating how many bombs are near them.


Other times, you will click on a tile with no bombs near them.

In this case, the area surrounding the tile will be revealed until it reaches a tile with a nearby bomb.


These numbered tiles are clues to identify where the bombs are placed.

Avoid the bombs and reveal all the safe tiles to win!
